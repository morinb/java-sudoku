java-sudoku
===========

Sudoku Solver in Java

Usage
===========
Just launch Main.java.
Sudoku are described with a line representation
<pre>
+-------+-------+-------+
| 7 . . | . . . | 9 4 . |
| . . 5 | 7 . 4 | . . 1 |
| . . . | . 3 . | . 8 . |
+-------+-------+-------+
| 9 . 8 | . . 7 | . . 4 |
| . . . | . . . | . . . |
| . 3 . | 5 . 1 | . 9 . |
+-------+-------+-------+
| . . . | 2 . . | . 6 . |
| 2 6 9 | . . . | . 5 3 |
| 1 . . | . 5 . | . . . |
+-------+-------+-------+
</pre>
becomes 
<pre>
"7.....94...57.4..1....3..8.9.8..7..4..........3.5.1.9....2...6.269....531...5...."
</pre>
